HOW TO NAVIGATE

🖱
Click any crate to send the object there. The robot at the current station picks it up, places it on the conveyor belt, and the robot at your chosen station receives it.
↔
Drag left / right to orbit the camera around the scene. Scroll to zoom in or out.
🏷
Station labels appear above each crate. Hover to highlight, click to visit. Green labels = already visited.
📋
Info panel on the right shows the project description after each delivery. The object starts at Station 1 — About Me.
